🎯 Learning Objectives

1️⃣ Understand Acid-Base Neutralization

Learn how acids and bases react to form salts and water. Understand that neutralization is an exothermic reaction where H⁺ ions from acids combine with OH⁻ ions from bases.

2️⃣ Measure pH Changes Accurately

Master the pH scale (0-14) and how to interpret pH values. Understand that each unit change represents a 10-fold change in H⁺ concentration, making precise measurement crucial in titrations.

3️⃣ Calculate Equivalence Points

Learn to determine the volume of titrant needed to neutralize an acid completely using stoichiometry. Understand how molarity, volume, and the number of protons/hydroxides affect the equivalence point calculation.

4️⃣ Select and Interpret Indicators

Discover how different indicators change color at different pH ranges. Learn to choose the appropriate indicator for your specific acid-base system to accurately detect the endpoint of titration.

5️⃣ Apply Quantitative Analysis

Develop skills to collect experimental data, analyze results, and draw meaningful conclusions. Use the recorded data to verify theoretical predictions and understand real-world applications of acid-base chemistry in industries like food and pharmaceuticals.
